## Best practices
- Gather data BEFORE any offer conversation starts — scrambling to
  research after an offer lands puts you at a real disadvantage.
- Weight recent data points over old ones; comp bands move faster than
  people expect, especially in fast-growing fields.
- Use `offer-comparison-matrix.md` once you actually have an offer in
  hand — this worksheet is for going into the conversation informed, not
  for comparing multiple offers.
