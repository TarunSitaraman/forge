## Best practices for this template
- Update the moment a status changes — a tracker updated weekly instead
  of in real time misses time-sensitive next actions (an OA deadline, an
  interview scheduling window).
- Log outcomes honestly, including rejections with a real note on what
  to change — this is the highest-value section for improving your next
  application cycle, not just a record.
- Prioritize by fit and deadline together, not just deadline — a
  well-fitted late-deadline role is often worth more focus than a
  poorly-fitted urgent one.
