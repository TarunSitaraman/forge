# Template: Weekly Review

*Guidance: this is a review, not a journal — it exists to catch drift
(stalled projects, slipping deadlines, an Inbox filling up) early. Keep
it short enough that you'll actually do it every week.*

---

# Week of {date}

## What shipped / got done

{Concrete outcomes, not activity — "shipped X," not "worked on X."}

## What didn't move, and why

{Honest accounting — if something's stalled, name the actual blocker.}

## Inbox / Forge maintenance

- [ ] Inbox reviewed and filed/cleared
- [ ] Any project ready to move to `Archive/`?
- [ ] Any reusable pattern from this week worth adding to `Technologies/`?

## Next week's focus

{The 1-3 things that matter most — not a full task list, the actual

## Flags

{Anything at risk — a slipping deadline, a dependency at risk, a decision
that needs to be made — surfaced now while there's still time to act.}

---

## Best practices for this template
- Keep this to under 10 minutes to fill out — a weekly review that takes
  an hour won't survive contact with a busy week.
- Be honest in "what didn't move" — a review that only records wins
  misses the entire point of catching drift early.
- Don't let this become a journal; if an entry is more reflection than
  status, it belongs elsewhere, not in Forge (see `START_HERE.md` on
  Forge's no-journaling principle).
