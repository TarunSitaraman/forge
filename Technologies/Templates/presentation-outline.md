## Best practices for this template
- Fill in "Single takeaway" before anything else — a presentation
  without one collapses into an unordered content dump.
- Every row in the Build table should state what the slide PROVES, not
  what it CONTAINS — that discipline keeps the deck argumentative rather
  than descriptive.
- Don't skip objection handling; unaddressed pushback derails Q&A far
  more than a slightly shorter deck does.
