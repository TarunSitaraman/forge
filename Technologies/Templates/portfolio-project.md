tech stack.*

---

## Best practices for this template
- Lead with the problem and your specific contribution, not the tech
  stack — a reviewer evaluating judgment cares more about decisions made
  than libraries used.
- Make "Key technical decisions" genuinely defensible — pick decisions
  you could explain and justify if asked about them in an interview.
- Be honest about "what I'd improve" — a portfolio piece that claims
  perfection reads as less credible than one showing continued thinking.
