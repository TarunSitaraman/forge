# Overview

## What Is This Certificate?

The IBM RAG and Agentic AI Professional Certificate is a 10-course Coursera
program teaching advanced applied AI engineering: building efficient RAG
pipelines, working with vector databases, integrating multimodal models
(text/image/audio/video), and designing autonomous multi-agent systems
using LangChain, LangGraph, CrewAI, AutoGen (AG2), BeeAI, and the Model
Context Protocol (MCP).

**Provider:** IBM (hosted on Coursera)
**Format:** Asynchronous, video + hands-on labs (Jupyter notebooks, watsonx)
**Duration:** ~8 weeks at ~3 hrs/week, ~109 hours total across 10 courses
**Capstone:** Yes — Course 10 is an open-ended engineering project, not a
guided tutorial

## Why Taking This

- RAG and agentic AI are now baseline expectations for AI engineering
  roles, not specialized skills — this closes a structured knowledge gap
  faster than piecing it together from scattered blog posts.
- Hands-on labs (not just video) force actually building pipelines and
  agents, not just recognizing the vocabulary.
- The capstone produces a real, portfolio-worthy artifact (an end-to-end
  agentic RAG system over a real corpus) rather than a toy exercise.
- Structured sequencing (RAG fundamentals before agentic AI) matches how
  the concepts actually build on each other — agentic RAG only makes
  sense once plain RAG is solid.
