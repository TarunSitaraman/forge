# Courses

*Structured learning material and skill-development systems.*

Courses contains organized, skill-focused learning material — trackers, coaching prompts, and structured progression designed to help you deliberately improve at specific engineering disciplines.

## What belongs here

- **Competitive-Programming/** — Skill-growth trackers for problem-solving, mental models for different problem classes (dynamic programming, graphs, strings, etc.), and 10 coaching prompts that help you identify weaknesses and improve. Not algorithm notes; progress trackers. When to use: training to become a better problem solver.
- **IBM-RAG-and-Agentic-AI/** — Knowledge-pack-style tracker for the IBM RAG and Agentic AI Professional Certificate (Coursera, 10 courses), structured like `Projects/smartresq/` (numbered docs + `_index.md` hub + bonus prep guide), scaled to a curriculum instead of a codebase: overview, curriculum map, per-phase track docs (RAG/vector DB, multimodal, agentic AI), capstone planning, glossary, granular progress tracker, roadmap, and a certification-prep guide. Course-specific implementation notes and lab progress live here; durable RAG/agent theory merges into `Technologies/Docs/` (`rag.md`, `vector-databases.md`, `langchain.md`, `ai-agents.md`) as it's learned — never duplicated in both places.

## What doesn't belong here

- Casual notes or passing thoughts about programming concepts (those belong in Reference/).
- Technical reference material or explanations (those belong in Technologies/Docs/).
- Reusable prompts and systems outside a structured course (those belong in Technologies/Prompt-Library/).
- Your actual project work (that belongs in Projects/).

## How to use this folder

Each sub-folder is a skill course with its own progression. Use the trackers to measure growth, the coaching prompts to identify and work through gaps. These are meant to be revisited over months or quarters, not consumed once.
