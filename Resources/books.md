# Books

*Books worth the real time investment of finishing — the highest-time-cost
resource in this module after courses. A book earns a place here for a
specific idea or framing that changed how you approach real work, not
because it's a widely-recognized title.*

---

## Admission criteria

Add a book only after finishing it (or a substantial, load-bearing
portion) and can state:
- The specific idea or mental model you still use from it — not "it's a
  classic" or "everyone recommends it."
- Who it's actually for — a book that reshaped how a senior engineer
  thinks about systems may be the wrong recommendation for someone just
  starting out, and vice versa.

## How to use this page

Reach for a book when you want depth and a developed argument over time
— for a fast answer, official docs or `Technologies/Docs/` will serve you
better than a whole book.
