# Number Theory Representative Problems

Core variants exercising Number Theory concepts.

## Variant 1: GCD & LCM
- Greatest common divisor (Euclidean algorithm)
- Least common multiple
- Extended Euclidean algorithm for linear Diophantine

## Variant 2: Prime Numbers
- Sieve of Eratosthenes O(n log log n)
- Prime checking
- Prime factorization

## Variant 3: Modular Arithmetic
- Modular exponentiation (fast power)
- Modular inverse
- Chinese Remainder Theorem

## Variant 4: Divisors & Factors
- Count divisors efficiently
- Sum of divisors
- Perfect numbers, amicable numbers

## Variant 5: Number Properties
- Fibonacci properties
- Catalan numbers
- Triangular numbers
