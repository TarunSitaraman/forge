# Archive

*Completed or abandoned projects, kept for record.*

When a project in `Projects/` finishes or is abandoned, move its entire
folder here as-is: `git mv Projects/<name> Archive/<name>`. Don't edit
or "clean up" content on the way in — the archive preserves what
actually happened, not a tidied retrospective.

**Rule:** nothing is created directly in `Archive/`. Everything here
arrived via `git mv` from `Projects/`.

If an archived project's pattern turns out to be reusable, extract it
into `Technologies/` explicitly — don't leave people trying to reuse things
by digging through the archive.
