## Best practices
- Review quarterly, on a fixed schedule — a career plan that's only
  revisited during a crisis (a bad review, a layoff scare) is reactive,
  not planned.
- Keep actions concrete and boundable; vague intentions ("build my
  brand") don't get checked off and don't get done.
- Be honest in "what's not working" — a career plan that only lists
  positives can't actually identify what needs to change.
