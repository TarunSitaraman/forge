## Best practices
- Build stories from real, specific memories — a story invented to fill
  a competency gap reads as fabricated under real follow-up questions.
- Re-rehearse (not re-read) each story before an interview cycle — the
  goal is fluent delivery, not a memorized script that sounds robotic.
- Update "last used" honestly; a story you haven't touched in 6 months
  needs a re-read before you rely on it again.
