## Best practices
- Fill this in the same day — contest-specific reasoning (why you
  chose to skip a problem, what confused you) decays fast from memory.
- Be honest about time management, not just problem-solving — pacing
  mistakes are just as fixable as pattern-recognition gaps, but only if
  you actually notice them.
- Feed unsolved problems from here into the Problem Journal for a full
  entry, and into post-solution analysis once you've solved them.
