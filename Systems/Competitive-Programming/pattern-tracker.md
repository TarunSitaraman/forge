## Best practices
- Rate yourself honestly on recognition speed vs. application confidence
  separately — it's common to recognize a pattern immediately but still
  fumble the implementation, or vice versa. Conflating them hides where
  to focus.
- Revisit "last practiced" dates periodically — a pattern untouched for
  months is worth a refresh problem even if your confidence rating is high.
- Cross-reference with the Mistake Tracker — a pattern with low
  application confidence often maps directly to a recurring mistake.
