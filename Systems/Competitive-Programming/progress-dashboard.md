## Best practices
- Update this on a fixed cadence (monthly, or after every N contests) —
  a dashboard updated only when things are going well isn't a real
  dashboard.
- Prioritize the unaided-solve ratio over raw rating in judging real
  improvement; rating can be noisy short-term, and hint-dependence is a
  more honest signal of whether independent problem-solving is actually
  getting better.
