## Best practices
- Write the "first read" section before attempting the problem, not
  after — retroactive honesty about your instincts is much weaker signal.
- Log unsolved problems too. A journal with only solved problems can't
  show you what actually trips you up.
- Feed a batch of entries into `Systems/Prompt-Library/Competitive-Programming/cp-weakness-finder.md`
  periodically — the aggregate pattern across entries is more useful than
  any single one.
