# Blogs

*Engineering blogs with a genuinely distinct voice or depth — writing
that changed how you think about a problem, not every blog post you've
ever bookmarked.*

---

## Admission criteria

Add a blog (or a specific standout post) only when you can state the
specific idea or framing it gave you that you still use. A blog that's
"generally good" without a specific memorable contribution isn't ready
to be curated yet.
