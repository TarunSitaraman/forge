# Research Papers

*Papers that changed how you think about a problem — not a reading list
of everything relevant to a field. Use
`Systems/Prompt-Library/Research/paper-critical-reading.md` to properly
evaluate a paper before deciding it earns a place here.*

---

## Admission criteria

Add a paper only when you can state, in one sentence, the specific idea
it gave you that you still reach for — not "it's foundational" or
"everyone cites it." If you haven't done a critical read (contribution +
evidence strength + limitations), do that first.

*(Seed this only from papers you've personally read critically — a
paper added because it's famous, without your own read, isn't curation,
it's forwarding someone else's list.)*

## How to use this page

Pair with `Systems/Prompt-Library/Research/` prompts:
`literature-review-synthesis.md` when comparing several papers,

whether it belongs here.
