# YouTube Channels

*Channels that teach durable mental models — not a channel you happened
to watch one video from. Video content changes creators, quality, and
relevance fast; validate personally before adding, and re-validate
periodically since a channel's quality can drift over its lifetime.*

---

## Admission criteria

Add a channel only when you can state:
- The specific kind of understanding it builds (e.g. "explains WHY a
  system is designed a certain way, not just how to use it").
- Whether it's evergreen (concepts) or time-sensitive (specific tool
  versions) — flag time-sensitive content so future-you knows to check
  for staleness.

## How to use this page

Reach for a channel here when you want a different explanation style
than official docs provide (visual, conversational) — not as a
replacement for the primary source in `official-documentation.md`.
