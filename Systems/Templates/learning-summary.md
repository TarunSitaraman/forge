## Best practices for this template
- Write this immediately after the learning happens, not days later —
  the specific gotchas are the first thing you forget.
- Keep it to what's genuinely non-obvious; a summary that just restates
  the official docs isn't earning its place in `Reference/`.
- File the finished note under `Reference/<topic>/`, not here — this
  template produces the note, `Reference/` is where it lives.
