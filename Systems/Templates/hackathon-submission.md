## Best practices for this template
- Lead with problem + outcome, not tech stack — judges scoring on impact
  or business viability skim past stack-first submissions.
- Be honest in "Challenges faced" — a submission that claims everything
  went smoothly reads as less credible, not more impressive.
- Keep the "How it works" section optional-reading — structure it so a
  time-pressed judge can skip straight to the demo link.
