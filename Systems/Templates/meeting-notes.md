# Template: Meeting Notes

*Guidance: meeting notes exist to make decisions and action items
findable later — not to transcribe the conversation. If a note doesn't
have at least one decision or action item, question whether the meeting
needed notes at all.*

---

## Best practices for this template
- Decisions and action items go at the top, discussion notes below —
  most readers only need the top section.
- Assign a real owner to every action item — an action with no owner
  reliably doesn't get done.
- Skip this template entirely for meetings with no decisions or actions
  (status syncs, brainstorms) — a lighter note or none at all is fine.
