# Template: Research Report

*Guidance: a research report exists to inform a decision or a body of
knowledge — it should lead with the answer, not build up to it. A reader
should get the conclusion from the first paragraph even if they read
nothing else.*

---

---

## Best practices for this template
- Lead with the answer — busy readers should get the conclusion from the
  top even if they never reach "Methodology."
- State confidence levels honestly per finding; not every finding in a
  report deserves equal trust.
- Don't organize findings source-by-source — that just reproduces
  separate summaries instead of synthesizing an answer (see
  `Systems/Prompt-Library/Research/literature-review-synthesis.md`).
