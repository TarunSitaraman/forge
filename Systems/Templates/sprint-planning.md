## Best practices for this template
- Set capacity honestly, accounting for real availability — planning
  against nominal full-time capacity when people have meetings, PTO, or
  on-call is the most common cause of sprint overcommitment.
- Track carryover explicitly over multiple sprints — a pattern of the
  same type of work carrying over signals an estimation or dependency
  problem worth fixing at the process level.
- Keep the sprint goal to one sentence; a sprint with five equally-weighted
  goals has no goal.
